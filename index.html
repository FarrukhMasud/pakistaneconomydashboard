Pakistan Economic Dashboard

An interactive dashboard of Pakistan's key economic indicators, built entirely from official government data — the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP), Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS), the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) and the Finance Division.

The dashboard covers:

  • Foreign exchange reserves and import cover
  • PKR exchange rate vs USD, EUR, GBP and CNY
  • Inflation — national, urban and rural CPI, SPI and WPI
  • Workers' remittances by source country
  • Monthly imports, exports and trade balance by country
  • Foreign direct investment (FDI)
  • IT, software and freelancing exports
  • FBR tax collection and targets
  • GDP growth and public finance
  • Federal and provincial budgets
